# VaultTech — Pitch Script
> Hurricane Hackathon 2026 · 3-minute demo pitch

---

## Opening (30 seconds) — Vision

"It's 2030. You don't just live in the physical world — you have a digital twin that exists 24/7 online. It's on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, data broker sites, news archives. It has your email, maybe your phone number, your address history.

The problem: most people have never met their digital twin. They don't know what it looks like, what it's saying about them, or what doors it's leaving open for identity thieves."

---

## Problem (20 seconds)

"Today alone, there are over 15 billion records exposed from data breaches. Your email is probably in at least one of them. You just don't know which one, or what that means for your security."

---

## Demo (90 seconds)

"Let me show you VaultTech."

[Open browser to GitHub Pages URL]

"I'm going to enter a name and email. Watch what happens."

[Type in demo name and email — use pre-prepared safe demo values]

"VaultTech is now scanning. It's pulling together your entire public face — every platform you exist on. It's checking breach databases for your email."

[Results appear]

"Here's your Public Face — every platform where you exist publicly, one click away. Here's your Exposure Report — [X] breaches found, here's exactly what data was exposed and when.

And here — your VaultScore."

[Point to badge]

"This is the number that matters. This tells you at a glance: how exposed are you? Not a list of technical jargon — a single, clear threat level."

[AI recommendations stream in]

"And VaultTech's AI is now generating personalised recommendations — specific actions, prioritised by urgency, to lock down your digital identity."

## Close

"VaultTech. Your digital identity, secured."

## Judge Q&A Prep

**Q: How do you get the profile data?**
A: We surface constructed search links — we're not scraping platforms, we're giving users one-click access to find themselves. The breach data comes from HaveIBeenPwned, the industry standard.

**Q: Is this secure — you're handling email addresses?**
A: Nothing is stored. All processing is client-side in the browser. We never send your data to our servers — because we don't have any. It's fully static.

**Q: What's the business model?**
A: Freemium. Free VaultScan for basic recon. VaultPro subscription for continuous monitoring, profile sync, and the VaultAgent.

**Q: How is this different from HaveIBeenPwned?**
A: HIBP tells you if you were breached. VaultTech tells you your complete digital exposure picture and tells you what to do about it. It's triage + treatment, not just diagnosis.
