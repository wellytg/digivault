The Vision

Know Your Shadow

Most people have never met their "digital twin" — the aggregate of their LinkedIn, social media, data broker entries, and breach records.

  • Fragmented Identity: Your persona is scattered across 10+ platforms.
  • Unseen Exposure: Over 15 billion records are exposed in data breaches today.
  • The Goal: Centralized "Ground Truth" for your digital presence.
The Solution

The DigiScan Engine

Identity Linkage

Correlates disparate social handles and emails into a single risk profile.

VaultScore™

A single, clear threat level score based on public exposure and known breaches.

AI Triage

Gemini-powered personalized security recommendations and prioritised action plans.

Zero-Trust Architecture

Client-side only. No data is ever sent to a backend server. Privacy by design.
